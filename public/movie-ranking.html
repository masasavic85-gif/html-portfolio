The best movies according to Masha

My top three movies of all time

The Fifth element

A visually explosive sci-fi adventure full of imagination, color and humor. A cult classic with a unique futuristic style.

The Godfather

A timeless masterpiece about family, power and loyalty. Slow, intense and perfectly crafted.

The Matrix

A groundbreaking sci-fi film that changed cinema forever. A mix of action, philosophy and questions about reality and freedom.
